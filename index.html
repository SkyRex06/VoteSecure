Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our voting platform.

How secure is the VoteSecure platform?

VoteSecure uses end-to-end encryption, two-factor authentication, and meets ISO 27001 security standards. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and we conduct regular third-party security audits to ensure the highest level of protection for your voting data.

Can I customize the voting interface with our branding?

Yes, all plans include customization options for adding your organization's logo, colors, and branding elements. The Professional and Enterprise plans offer additional white-labeling features, including custom domains and complete UI customization.

What types of voting methods are supported?

VoteSecure supports multiple voting methods including simple majority, ranked choice, approval voting, cumulative voting, and weighted voting. You can also create custom voting methods based on your organization's specific requirements.

Is VoteSecure compliant with regulations like GDPR?

Yes, VoteSecure is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant data protection regulations. We provide data processing agreements, and our platform includes features to help you maintain compliance, such as data export and deletion capabilities.

Can I integrate VoteSecure with our existing systems?

Yes, VoteSecure offers API access (Enterprise plan) and pre-built integrations with popular platforms like Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Slack, and major CRM systems. Our Professional and Enterprise plans include SSO options for seamless authentication.
